Building the team

From the beginning, my goal was to build a team that was ready for anything.

At most companies, design is decentralized. Designers are organized into functional groups like marketing or product; there's no community of practice across the board, and career paths are different for each function.

My vision for the design team was a centralized one: all the designers would be on the same team. My hypothesis is that this would result in a much stronger community of practice and a more consistent user experience.

Building the team in this way allowed me to attract great design talent, increase collaboration, deepen our community of practice, and create a stronger sense of belonging.
